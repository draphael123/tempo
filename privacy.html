TEMPO

Privacy Policy

Effective 7 July 2026

Short version: TEMPO does not collect, transmit, or share any of your information. Everything you enter stays on your own device.

What we collect

Nothing is sent to us or to anyone else. TEMPO has no account system, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers. We — the developers — never receive your data and cannot see it.

Where your information is stored

Everything you record in TEMPO — your medications, dose logs, symptom check-ins, meals, falls, wellness entries, exercise, movement-test results, emergency card, and notes — is stored only in local storage on your own device. It never leaves the device unless you choose to export or share it yourself.

Notifications

Dose reminders are scheduled locally on your device. They are generated on the device itself and are not routed through any server.

Health information

TEMPO is a personal tracking aid, not a medical device, and it is not intended to diagnose or treat any condition. The health-related information you enter is under your control at all times. Because it is stored only on your device, protecting the device (for example with a screen lock) protects that information.

Sharing and export

TEMPO includes optional features that let you share your own data — for example printing or saving a report as a PDF, or sharing your emergency card. These actions happen only when you initiate them, and the information goes only where you send it.

Data deletion

You can erase all stored data at any time from the app’s settings (“Erase all data”), or by uninstalling the app. Because nothing is stored on our side, there is nothing for us to delete.

Children

TEMPO is intended for adults managing their own or a family member’s medications and is not directed at children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date.
